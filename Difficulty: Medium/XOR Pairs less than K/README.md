<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/count-pairs-having-bitwise-xor-less-than-k/1">XOR Pairs less than K</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="user-select: auto;"><p data-pm-slice="0 0 []" style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">Given an array <strong style="user-select: auto;">arr[]</strong> and an integer <strong style="user-select: auto;">k,</strong> we need to count the number of pairs from the given array such that the Bitwise XOR of each pair is less than k.</span></p>
<p style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Input: </strong>arr = [1, 2, 3, 5], k = 5 <strong style="user-select: auto;"><br style="user-select: auto;"></strong><strong style="user-select: auto;">Output:</strong> 4<strong style="user-select: auto;">
Explanation: </strong>Bitwise XOR of all possible pairs that satisfy the given conditions are:<br style="user-select: auto;"></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">arr[0] ^ arr[1] = 1 ^ 2 = 3 </span><br style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">arr[0] ^ arr[2] = 1 ^ 3 = 2 </span><br style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">arr[0] ^ arr[3] = 1 ^ 5 = 4 </span><br style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">arr[1] ^ arr[2] = 2 ^ 3 = 1 <br style="user-select: auto;"></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">Therefore, the required output is 4.</span></pre>
<pre style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Input: </strong>arr[] = [3, 5, 6, 8], k = 7 <strong style="user-select: auto;"><br style="user-select: auto;"></strong><strong style="user-select: auto;">Output: </strong>3<br style="user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Explnation: </strong>Bitwise XOR of all possible pairs that satisfy the given conditions are:<br style="user-select: auto;"></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">arr[0] ^ arr[1] = 6<br style="user-select: auto;"></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">arr[0] ^ arr[2] = 5<br style="user-select: auto;"></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">arr[1] ^ arr[2] = 3<br style="user-select: auto;"></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">Therefore, the required output is 3.&nbsp;</span></pre>
